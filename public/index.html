Welcome to Letter Punk

Friendly, familiar, and a little freer than classic Letter Boxed.

  • Tap a letter to add it, or type it on your keyboard. Consecutive letters must come from different sides of the board.
  • Tap the same letter twice in a row to double it — double letters are welcome here.
  • Each new word must start with the last letter of the previous word — unless Free Chain mode is on (Settings), which drops that requirement entirely.
  • Words need 3+ letters and must be real dictionary words.
  • Use every letter on the board to solve it. There's no word limit, so take your time.
  • Undo Letter and Undo Word fix mistakes as you go.

Full rules, scoring details, and the story behind Letter Punk: jmajerus.github.io/letter-punk.
